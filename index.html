AI in General

AI is a more recent development in history, and it allows people to due many things with minimal effort, such as drive, solve questions, talk, etc. But one of the most controversial things it is being used for is AI art.

Current Uses of AI Art

AI Art is currently being used for multiple things, including

  • Helping Artists Finish Making Art
  • Making Full Pieces Of Art
  • Making Memes
  • Marketing For Companies

How AI Art May Impact Future Jobs

AI art will have a massive impact on the job market for artists, with multiple countries such as Rovio using it for their marketing and games. This has sparked outrage amongst artists for being replaced by robots, and rightly so, as the AI art itself isn't even that good to begin with.
